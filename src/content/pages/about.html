About

Mia Harper Photography

Welcome to the visual odyssey of Mia Harper, a Detroit-born photographer with a passion for capturing the untold stories of our city. Through the lens of her camera, Mia reveals the raw beauty and unfiltered resilience of Detroit—a place she calls home. Growing up surrounded by the echoes of Motown and the hum of automotive innovation, Mia's journey into photography was a natural evolution. Armed with her grandmother's vintage film camera, Mia embarked on a visual exploration, discovering the intricate dance between decay and renewal within the city's streets.

Mia's photography is a testament to the soul of Detroit, where each image is a brushstroke in the canvas of a city in perpetual metamorphosis. Whether she's uncovering the hidden poetry in abandoned structures or capturing the spirit of local communities, Mia's work transcends the visual, offering a narrative that speaks to the heart of Detroit's collective story. Join Mia on this visual journey through the lens—a celebration of Detroit's resilience, creativity, and the enduring beauty found in the midst of change.
